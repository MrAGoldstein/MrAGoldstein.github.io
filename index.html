About Me
While at high school, I was in the Computer Science Academy, later renamed the Computer Science Magnet Program, located at Freehold High School. The Computer Science Academy specializes in math and computer science. During my junior year, I was asked by one of my teachers if I would be willing to tutor a freshman in both geometry and the intro to programming class. Half-way through the year, Computer Science Academy freshman switch from geometry into discrete math. The next year I continued tutoring the same student and also was asked to tutor a freshman again, so I was then tutoring a sophomore and a freshman in A lgebra 2, Geometry, Discrete Math, and two computer science classes. Tutoring showed me that what I really wanted to do with my life is teach. I am currently a double major in mathematics and secondary education with a minor in computer science. While in college, I have been tutoring Calculus 1 and 2, Computer Science 1 and 2, and Linear Algebra.
